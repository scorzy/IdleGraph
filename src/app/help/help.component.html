HOW TO PLAY

Idle Graph is an Idle Incremental. The main goal is to reach enough main currency to prestige. When prestige you will star a new semi random Graph and you get some skill points.

Graph In this game there is a tree of resource generators. The root node is your main currency, every node has a level, that is the distance from Main, sarting from 1. A -> b implies that A make B Main node, leafs and other node have different colors. Buy With the Buy button you can buy more node, by buying node you also get a "bought bonus" that increase productivity. Producer With the Producer button you can buy a producer of the current node. Ticks Nodes production is based on /tick, not os "per second". You can increase your tick per second to maximize production. Sacrifice You can sacrifice node of level greater than 3 to gain a production bonus. Sacrifice will decrease the quantity of all node produced by this to zero, recursive. Sacrifice bonus is based on level and quantity of sacrificed nodes and level of this current node. New sacrifice bonus will substitute your previous sacrifice bonus, you can only sacrifice if the new sacrifice bonus is greater of actual bonus.
